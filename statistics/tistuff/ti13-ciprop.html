TO CALCULATE A CONFIDENCE INTERVAL FOR A PROPOTION ON THE TI

Suppose that a simple random sample of n=100 subjects included x=55 special subjects. We calculate a 95% confidence interval estimate for the proportion p of special subjects.
  1. First press STAT and then press the right arrow twice. You will see a menu for statistical tests.

  2. Scroll down until you see tests whose names include ``Interval'' or ``Int''

  3. For a proportion interval, we want 1-PropZInt. Select this and you will see

  4. Enter $x$, $n$, and your confidence level

  5. Select ``Calculate'' and press ENTER
