ENTERING DATA IN A LIST ON THE TI

  1. Press the STAT button.
  2. Pressing the STAT button will bring up this dialogue:

    Press ENTER to select ``1: Edit...''
  3. You should now see several lists

    You may move between the lists with the left/right arrows. Enter your data in list L1 by typing your numbers and pressing ENTER between the numbers.

    You can move up and down a list to correct errors with the up/down arrows.
  4. Press 2nd-QUIT when you are done entering data.
