TO DRAW A HISTOGRAM ON THE TI

  1. Enter your data in a list. We will use L1.

  2. Press 2nd-STAT PLOT

  3. The STAT PLOT button will bring up this dialogue:

    Press ENTER to select Plot1
  4. Plot1 may initially look like this:

    Use the arrows to move to ``On'' and press ENTER to turn the plot on.
  5. Use the arrows to move to the little graph that looks like a histogram. Press ENTER. Your calculator should now look like:

  6. Press the ZOOM button

  7. You will see the ZOOM menu:

    Scroll down to select the option ``ZoomStat''

  8. The TI will select a class width and draw a histogram:
