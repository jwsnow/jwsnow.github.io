CLEARING A LIST ON THE TI

  1. Follow STAT-EDIT as if to edit the list.

    Move to the list you want to clear. In this case, we will clear L1.
  2. Press the up arrow until the heading L1 is highlighted

  3. Press CLEAR then ENTER.

WHERE DID MY LIST GO?

It is very important when you clear a list to press CLEAR and not DEL. If you press DEL, you will delete the list. To bring the list back, you need to reset your memory?
