TO DRAW A BOXPLOT ON THE TI

  1. Enter your data into a list. We will use L1.

  2. Press 2nd-STAT PLOT
  3. Press ENTER to select Plot1
  4. If necessary, press ENTER to turn on Plot1.
  5. Use the arrows to maneuver to the plot that looks like a boxplot.

  6. Make sure the Xlist corresponds to where you entered data.
  7. Press ZOOM
  8. Select ZoomStat
