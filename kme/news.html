2019 Mach Award
Congratulations to Dr. Rhonda McKee, recipient of the George R. Mach Distinguished Service Award. Dr. McKee has served as corresponding secretary of the Missouri Beta chapter for more than 33 years. She also served at the national level for 18 years, including a term as National President. Congratulations, Dr. McKee!
